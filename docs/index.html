About Me

Hello! My name is Nathaniel Tupaz, and I'm a 21-year-old BSIT student with a passion for photography. I began my journey with mobile photography, capturing moments with just my phone. As my skills and interest grew, I transitioned into DSLR photography to take my work to the next level.

Over the years, I've gained experience in various types of shoots, including condo unit photography, car photography, product photography, as well as street and portrait photography. My goal is to continue honing my craft, exploring new techniques, and capturing stunning images that tell a story.
